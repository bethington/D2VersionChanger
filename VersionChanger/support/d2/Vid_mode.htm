    How do I change the Diablo II video mode:

      From your Desktop, click on Start --> Programs --> Diablo II --> Video Test.
      This will test your current hardware configuration, then allow you to switch your game's video mode.


      If the chosen video mode is not working correctly, contact your video card manufacturer for their latest drivers.


      Diablo II supports Glide™ and Direct 3D™ compatible video cards with at least 8MB of video RAM. Direct 3D™ requires 64 MB of system RAM.
      The screen area for Diablo II is set to 640x480. When on Battle.net through Diablo II the screen area is set to 800x600.
