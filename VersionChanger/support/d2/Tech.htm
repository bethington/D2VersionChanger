    Before contacting the Blizzard Entertainment technical support department please find the following information:
    • Your computer manufacturer
    • Which operating system you're using, and the version
    • How much RAM (memory) you have
    • The make and model of your sound and video cards
    • Microsoft System information: Msinfo32
    Please include this information in any support requests that you make.
